Welcome to my page

My name is Erica, and I'm an aspiring web developer. I feel its unexpected of me to be entering this field as my educational and career backgrounds are not technical.The majority of my background has been in customer service. As someone who has worked in various industries, from e-commerce to brick-and-mortar retail, technology had always played a significant role. Whether it be software for tracking orders, processing insurance quotes, or for analyzing sales projections, the effectiveness of all of these tools can either make or break a company's success. I've always been intrigued by the notion that someone built these programs.On a more personal level, my husband also happens to be a software engineer. Time and time again, I've seen him develop game-changing software solutions at numerous companies over the years, most recently at his own company - a Y Combinator-funded startup. I feel lucky because I've procured firsthand knowledge of what it's like to traverse the startup space.With 8 years of customer service experience and seeing the importance of software at every company I've worked at, I feel compelled to learn more about software development. I have chosen to pursue web development because web presence is essential in most of the businesses that we rely on today. I look forward to playing an active role in the production and maintenance of a business's web technology stack one day.

I have worked on various projects that show my expansive skill set. By working within several marketplaces I have shown adaptability. My primary goal with all projects is to achieve the highest level of functionality and presentability while meeting the customer's needs.
